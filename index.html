    FAQ

    Quick answers

    What is “Japanese yoga”?

    It draws on meridian theory and seasonal balance. Expect functional stretches, breathwork and sequences that support digestion, circulation and posture.

    Do I need experience?

    No — complete beginners are welcome. You’ll always get options for your body.

    Can you support Japanese speakers?

    Yes. Jane can provide simple Japanese support as needed, and the Japanese page has full details.
